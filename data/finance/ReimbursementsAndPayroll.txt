Finance Policies

1. Expense Reimbursements:
   - Travel reimbursement: Flights and hotels must be booked via the company portal.
   - Food allowance during business travel: ₹1,000 per day.
   - Claims must be submitted within 30 days of expense.

2. Payroll:
   - Salaries are credited on the last working day of the month.
   - Form-16 is available on the Finance portal every April.

3. Training and Certification Reimbursement:
   - Employees can claim up to ₹50,000 per year for skill development courses.
   - Requires manager approval before enrollment.

4. IT Asset Purchase:
   - Personal purchase of IT equipment for work use (monitors, keyboards) can be reimbursed up to ₹10,000 per year.

5. Finance Service Request SLAs:
   - Reimbursement approval: 7 working days
   - Payroll queries: 3 working days
   - Tax queries: 5 working days
